Indigenous Languages across Australia

This visualisation aims to highlight the wide variety and significant distribution of Australian Indigenous languages across the country.

Australian Indigenous languages are a critical aspect of the nation's cultural heritage, with hundreds of languages spoken by communities spread across diverse regions.

We aim to shed light on both the diversity and the challenges of preserving these languages in the modern era.

Created by Jeffrey Yan

Sources: Collated census data from .idcommunity

Auslang dataset from the Australian Institute of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Studies (AIATSIS)
